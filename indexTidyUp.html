Longbridge Lodge provides 24 hour supported living for adult mental health service users wishing to live in the community.

We provide a friendly, therapeutic environment and specialise in promoting social inclusion, independance and choice

The long-term aim is to prepare users for a "step down" to total independent living in the commuity whilst allowing for their disablities
